# Code of Conduct

Nodo contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, direct, and security-minded.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific in technical feedback.
- Discuss evidence, tradeoffs, and protocol risk clearly.
- Respect maintainers' responsibility to reject unsafe changes.
- Keep disagreements focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private vulnerability details before maintainers can respond.
- Pressuring contributors to bypass tests or safety checks.
- Misrepresenting the project's readiness status.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
